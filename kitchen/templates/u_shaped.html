Design Tips & Suggestions

Keep the Corners Functional

Use magic corners or carousel units to optimize otherwise wasted corner spaces in a U shaped kitchen.

Add Task Lighting

Under-cabinet lights help brighten up prep zones and enhance visibility across countertops.

Open One End for Flow

Keep one side of the U layout open to adjoining areas like the dining room for better movement and integration.
