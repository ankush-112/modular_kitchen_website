What is a G-Shaped Kitchen?

The G-Shaped kitchen is an extension of the U-shaped layout, adding a peninsula or partial fourth wall. It provides extra counter space and creates a semi-enclosed cooking zone ideal for multitasking.

Enhanced Counter Space

Perfect for families or hosts who love to cook and entertain simultaneously.

Partial Partitioning

Allows for visual separation from the dining or living area without isolation.

Additional Seating

Can incorporate a breakfast bar or seating zone on the extended arm.
